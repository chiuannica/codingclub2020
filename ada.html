Vietnamese Fan Dancing

In Vietnam, fan dancing is called, vũ phiến. It is performed in vietnamese theatres and is performed in a group, rarely solo.

We usually use large, soft colorful fans to represent beauty, grace, and nature. When performing, we wear ao dai, traditional vietnamese gowns worn with trousers.
